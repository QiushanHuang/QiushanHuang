### 🔬 Research Focus

Current research centers on molecular dynamics simulations of liquid crystals, especially liquid crystal elastomers, and phase separation in soft matter. Additional interests include reproducible LAMMPS workflows, research automation, and hybrid molecular dynamics plus machine learning frameworks for simulation and analysis.
